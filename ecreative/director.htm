B.R.Gupta- Founder and Chairman
Mr. B.R.Gupta, a self-made man, established a small trucking business in the 1968. What started of as a small venture soon multiplied into an extensive business group, now offering complete logistic solutions (surface transportation), and other services like Corporate Finance and Bill Discounting through its group Companies, which also signifies our operational capabilities and financial soundness. With his able leadership and vision, he nurtured the nascent organization into a Nation wide Transport Company catering to the very need of the customers nationally. Complete trust and transparency of services truly imbibed the culture of business partnership with clients in the organization.
V.K.Gupta- Managing Director  
 

By 1980 when the company was still growing the 2nd generation had already been groomed to take the reigns of the business. Mr. Vijay Kumar Gupta joined the Family business in 1981 started by his father. Through his innovative methods and dynamic leadership has transformed PRC to what it is today. From a one man in one office in 1968, the company has steadily grown into a sizeable business of about INR 50 crores and 35 offices by 2003 under his able leadership. PRC today boasts of having an integrated computer network at all major branches for complete customer satisfaction.
   
Yashu Gupta- Director

A young and dynamic person who has just completed his graduation in Mumbai and joined the family business started by his grand-father with a vision to take the Company into further heights. He has already started to look after the day to day affairs of the Company in a big way in consultation with his father Mr. Vijay Kumar Gupta.

Sushma Gupta - Director
