IMPORTS  
How could Creative’s creativity have any geographical limitations - the maxim goes......"The sky is the limit"..... similarly for the entrepreneurs of Creative – “The globe is the limit”. After claiming a consolidated position in the national market, we took a step further and went global thereby seeking to explore the intricacies of the international market. Creative has indulged in the imports of various products like Memories, GPS, Pen drives, Rams and the growth graph in this arena has shown an impressive upward trend.
  
 
   
The import value for the year 2004-05 was Rs.73 lacs
And for the year 2005-06 was Rs.4 crores 36 lacs indicating a growth index of approximately 300%.
We also take up back to back orders for special customer requirements & also do $ billings in STPI units.
   
  The list of documents required for import clearance  
IEC / PAN copy  
Invoice  
Packing List  
AWB / Bill of Lading  
Authority letter  
GATT Declarations ( Sign & Seal )  
R.Form / N Form ( Sign & Seal )  
BMC undertaking on consignee’s letter for N Form  
Literature / Catalogue / Certificate of analysis / Technical Write-up  
Certificate of Origin  
Self Explanation Letter  
   
Additional Docs for EPCG Scheme  
   
EPCG License / Customs File No.  
Bond / Bank Guarantee ( As per license condition )  
Duty Saved License – Manual B/E, CIF License – EDI B/E  
   

Additional Docs for STP Scheme

  
   
Software Technology Parks of India Import Certificate  
Green Card ( Self attaested )  
License under section 58 of Custom Act 1962 for Private Bonded Warehouse ( Self Attested )  
License for manufacture of Goods in bond issued under section 65 of Custom Act 1962 ( Self Attested )  
Letter of STPI intimating the acceptance of Legal agreement ( Self Attested )  
List of Capital Goods ( Self Attested )  
Procurement Certificate with custom / central excise seal cover  
100% EOU Bond Custom Registration No. ( Acc., JNPT,  Mumbai )  
   
Additional Docs for High Sea Sale  
   
IEC / PAN copy of Buyer / Seller  
Invoice / Commercial Invoice  
Packing List  
AWB / Bill of Lading ( Duly signed by Buyer / Seller )  
High Seas Sale Agreement ( Duly Notary / Duly Signed by Buyer / Seller )  
Buyer / Seller Letter to Asst. Customs.  
Blank Letterhead  
Literature, Catalogue, Certificate of analysis, Technical Write-up  
Certificate of Origin  
High Seas Sale Agreement date should be middle of Departure & Arrival date.
